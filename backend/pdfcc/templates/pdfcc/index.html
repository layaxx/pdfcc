Analyse and Change Colors in your PDF

This app analyses a given PDF and shows every color that is used in it (apart from images). Colors are ordered by the amount of pages they appear on. You can then specify new colors to replace the old ones. Please specify your colors as 6-digit hex-code.

About this project

PDF Color Changer...

... can:

  • analyze colors used in a PDF
  • substitute colors used in a PDF
  • process PDFs up to 15MB in size

... cannot:

  • analyze or substitute colors used
    inside an image embedded in a PDF

this is a proof of concept.
